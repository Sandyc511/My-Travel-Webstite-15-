About

The Eiffel Tower, located in the heart of Paris, is one of France's most iconic landmarks and one of the world's most famous buildings. The Eiffel Tower was built in 1889 to commemorate the Exposition Universelle 1889 (World's Fair).Designer is Gustave Eiffel. It was initially criticized as "ugly"Now it has become a symbol of France. From controversial architecture to a world wonder.

🥐 Croissant

👉 The surrounding area boasts numerous cafes and upscale restaurants, creating a very French atmosphere.

Transportation

Getting to the Eiffel Tower is very convenient:

🚇 Metro: Take the Paris Metro to Bir-Hakeim station

🚆 RER Train: Get off at Champ de Mars station

🚌 Bus: Multiple routes available

🚶 Walking: Stroll along the Seine River; very romantic.

👉 It's recommended to go in the evening to enjoy both daytime and nighttime views.
